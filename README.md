#  Sabarmati Human & Waste Detection System

> **Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025 – Hardware Edition Finalist Project**

##  Overview

The **Sabarmati Human & Waste Detection System** is an AI-powered smart riverfront monitoring solution developed as part of the **Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025 – Hardware Edition Finals**. The project addresses critical challenges faced by the **Sabarmati Riverfront** and other urban water bodies by combining Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, IoT, drones, and smart surveillance technologies.

Our system enables real-time detection of **floating waste**, **human presence in restricted or dangerous zones**, and other environmental anomalies to improve river cleanliness, public safety, and operational efficiency.

---

##  Problem Statement

Urban riverfronts face several challenges, including:

* Floating plastic and solid waste accumulation
* Water pollution and environmental degradation
* Delayed identification of people in unsafe or restricted areas
* Manual monitoring with limited real-time insights
* Lack of integrated smart surveillance systems

This project aims to provide a scalable and cost-effective solution for intelligent riverfront monitoring.

##  How It Works

1. Capture live video using a camera or drone.
2. Process frames using the AI detection model.
3. Detect:

   * Humans
   * Floating waste
4. Mark detected objects with bounding boxes.
5. Store detection data with timestamps and location.
6. Generate alerts for relevant authorities when required.
7. Display results on the monitoring dashboard.
